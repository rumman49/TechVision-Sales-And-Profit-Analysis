# 📊 TechVision Sales & Profit Analysis

> **A deep-dive Excel dashboard analyzing ₹21.4 Crore in sales and profit for a leading consumer electronics company across Indian regions.**

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

TechVision is a simulated consumer electronics company operating across India. This project digs into real-world business challenges — uncovering which products drive profit, which regions underperform, and where the growth opportunities lie.

Built entirely in **Microsoft Excel**, this dashboard transforms raw transactional data into actionable business intelligence that any stakeholder can understand at a glance.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

The management team at TechVision needed clarity on:
- Which product categories generate the most revenue vs. profit?
- How do different Indian regions compare in terms of sales performance?
- Are there seasonal trends affecting revenue cycles?
- Which segments need urgent attention to improve margins?

## 🔍 Key Insights

- 💰 **Total Sales:** ₹21.4 Crore analyzed across all regions
- 🗺️ **Regional Breakdown:** Performance compared across North, South, East & West India
- 📦 **Product Category Analysis:** Identified top-performing vs. underperforming SKUs
- 📉 **Profit Margin Deep Dive:** Flagged low-margin products eating into profitability
- 📅 **Time-based Trends:** Monthly and quarterly sales patterns visualized
